About the Project

Construction Safety AI is an innovative solution aimed at enhancing workplace safety on construction sites. By leveraging advanced AI, the system detects Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) violations in real-time and sends email alerts to ensure compliance and safety.

YOLOv11 Model

The core of the system is powered by YOLOv11, a state-of-the-art object detection model from Ultralytics. YOLOv11 efficiently identifies PPE violations, such as missing hardhats, masks, or safety vests, in video streams with high accuracy and speed, making it ideal for real-time applications.

Data Collection, Training, and Augmentation

The model was trained on datasets sourced from open platforms like Roboflow and Kaggle, containing annotated images of construction workers. Data preprocessing involved cleaning and standardizing images, while augmentation was performed using Roboflow to enhance dataset diversity, ensuring robust performance across varied site conditions.

Frontend Development

The frontend is built using Flask, HTML, and CSS, delivering a sleek, user-friendly interface. Flask templates power the responsive design, with a dark theme, Inter font for readability, and intuitive controls for video uploads, webcam toggling, and CCTV stream integration, ensuring seamless interaction for safety supervisors.
